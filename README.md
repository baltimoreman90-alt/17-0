# 17–0

An all-time NFL roster-building game. Draft a 14-man starting lineup of legends from across seven decades, balance the five phases of football, and chase the only record that matters: a perfect **17–0** season and a Lombardi Trophy.

Inspired by the all-time-team format of [82-0](https://www.82-0.com/), rebuilt from scratch for football.

**▶ Play it:** _add your GitHub Pages URL here once it's live (e.g. https://yourusername.github.io/17-0/)_

## How it works

- **Draft a full lineup (14 starters).** Offense: 1 QB · 1 RB · 3 WR · 1 TE · 2 OL. Defense: 2 DL · 1 LB · 2 CB · 1 S.
- **Each pick locks a position** and the board spins you a random **era** (1960s–2020s). You draft the best available legend from that decade. Eras can repeat across your roster; players can't.
- **Five phases win games:** Offense, Defense, Trenches, Playmaking, and Football IQ. Your team total in each phase is the sum of all 14 starters.
- **Your weakest phase caps your ceiling.** To go 17–0 you have to be elite at everything — one neglected phase and you get exposed all season.
- **Make the playoffs (9–8 or better)** and chase a title. Run the table after a 17–0 regular season and you finish a perfect 20–0.
- **Two modes:** *Classic* (stats and phase bars visible) and *Field Vision* (hidden — draft from memory and gut).

## Running it

It's a single self-contained HTML file — no build step, no dependencies (other than Google Fonts loaded over the web). Just open `index.html` in any modern browser, or host it as a static site.
